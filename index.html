About Us

What Makes Our Foods Special?



At Dosirak by Sam’s Korean Food, every meal is a celebration of Korean tradition and flavor. We serve handcrafted dosirak (Korean lunchboxes) packed with homestyle recipes, premium ingredients, and bold, balanced flavors. From perfectly marinated bulgogi to crispy jeon and vibrant kimchi, each box is made fresh with love and attention to detail—just like mom used to make. Whether you're new to Korean cuisine or a lifelong fan, our food brings the heart of Korea straight to your table.
